Your voter journey

From eligible to voted

Dates shown are examples — actual election dates are announced by the Election Commission of India.

1
~6 months before polling
📋

Register to vote

Submit Form 6 at voters.eci.gov.in. You need proof of age, address, and a passport-size photo.

✓ Completed
2
~2 weeks before polling
🗺️

Find your polling booth

Search the electoral roll to confirm your name, serial number, and assigned polling station.

✓ Completed
3
Polling day
🗳️

Cast your vote

Go to your assigned booth, get your identity verified, press the blue button on the Ballot Unit for your chosen candidate, and verify your VVPAT slip (visible for ~7 seconds).

● Upcoming
4
After polling
🏆

Results day

Vote counting is conducted in the presence of candidates and their agents. VVPAT slips of 5 randomly selected booths per constituency are cross-verified with the electronic count.
